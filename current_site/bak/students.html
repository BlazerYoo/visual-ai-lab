Postdocs
Please contact me if you're interested in doing a postdoc in my lab.

Princeton students (current or incoming)
If you are a PhD, Master's or undergraduate student at Princeton interested in working with me, don't hesitate to reach out. I am actively recruiting students starting as early as summer 2017.

Prospective Princeton students
If you're a student interested in studying at Princeton, please go ahead and apply directly to the Princeton Computer Science department. It's helpful to mention ``Computer Vision'' or ``AI/ML'' as an area of interest, and/or include my name in your research statement. I'm unfortunately unable to respond to individual requests regarding admission.

Visiting students
Unfortunately I am currently unable to accommodate visiting students. Please check back here as this may change in future years.
